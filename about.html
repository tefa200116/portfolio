Hi! I'm Estefania Gomez.

I'm a designer and hand lettering artist based in Detroit, but I'll always be a California gal at heart.

I picked up hand lettering as a hobby while studying advertising at the School of Visual Arts, and over the next few years, what started as a creative outlet turned into something so much more. I leveraged a few clever passion projects into a thriving freelance business!

My work has been featured in campaigns for brands like Target, Starbucks, and Adobe, and I've had the honor of creating murals and installations that bring joy to communities across the country. But what I love most is the human connection behind every project‚Äîworking with passionate people to bring their visions to life through color, typography, and a whole lot of creativity.

I know from experience that building a creative career can be a challenging and overwhelming endeavor, which is why I love sharing what I've learned with others through my online courses and free resources on my blog. When I'm not designing or teaching, you'll find me exploring flea markets for vintage treasures, experimenting with new recipes in the kitchen, or planning my next travel adventure.

Thanks for stopping by! I'm so glad you're here. Whether you're a fellow creative, a potential client, or just someone who appreciates good design, I'd love to connect with you.
